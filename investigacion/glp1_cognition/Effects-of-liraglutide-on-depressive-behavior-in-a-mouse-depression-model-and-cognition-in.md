## Abstract / Summary
: We investigated the effects of liraglutide, a glucagon-like peptide-1 (GLP-1) agonist, on a depression- 
like phenotype in mice exposed to chronic unpredictable stress (CUS). Learning and memory were also assessed 
using the Morris water maze (MWM) test.
