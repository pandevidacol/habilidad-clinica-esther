Med.2016;375:1834-44; Husain. N Engl J Med.2019;381:841-51). In order to investi-
gate the potential effects of GLP-1 RA on dementia in a clinical setting, a post-hoc anal-
ysis was conducted based on pooled data from three CVOTs.
