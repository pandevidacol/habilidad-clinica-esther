## Abstract / Summary
Background Diabetes is an independent risk factor for cognitive impairment. We aimed to investigate the association 
between the glucagon-like peptide-1 (GLP-1) receptor agonist dulaglutide and cognitive impairment as an exploratory 
analysis within the Researching Cardiovascular Events With a Weekly Incretin in Diabetes (REWIND) trial.
