mediated by central GLP-1 receptor (GLP-1R). Little information is available, however, about the subcellular localization

immunocytochemistry was performed at light and electron microscopic levels. The highest density of GLP-1R-immunoreac-
tivity was observed in the circumventricular organs and regions in the vicinity of these areas like in the arcuate nucleus (ARC) 
and in the nucleus tractus solitarii (NTS). In addition, GLP-1R-immunreactive (IR) neuronal profiles were also observed in a
