## Abstract / Summary

receptors located in key energy balance regulating CNS areas, the hypothalamus or the
hindbrain. Moreover, GLP-1 can reduce reward derived from food and motivation to obtain

on the hindbrain, the nucleus of the solitary tract (NTS), GLP-1 receptors (GLP-1R).
