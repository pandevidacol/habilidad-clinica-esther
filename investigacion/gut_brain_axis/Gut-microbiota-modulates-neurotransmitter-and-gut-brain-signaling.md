centrally. Accumulating evidence suggests that the gut microbiota modulates intestinal neurotransmitter meta-
bolism and gut-to-brain signaling, shedding light on the crucial role of the gut microbiota in brain function and
the pathogenesis of various neuropsychiatric diseases, such as major depression disorder (MDD), anxiety,
addiction and Parkinson’s disease (PD). Despite the exciting findings, the mechanisms underlying the modulation
