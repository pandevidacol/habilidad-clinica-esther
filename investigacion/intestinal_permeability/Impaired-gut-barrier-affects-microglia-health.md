Impaired gut barrier affects microglia health
As researchers uncover new roles for the microbiome in health and disease, recent studies have emerged linking 
the microbiome to aspects of aging. Mossad and colleagues demonstrate that a microbiome-mediated disruption 
of the intestinal barrier, associated with aging, can contribute to dysfunction of microglia in mice.
John W. Bostick and Sarkis K. Mazmanian
